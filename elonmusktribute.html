Elon Musk

The man who will take us to Mars

A time line of Elon Musk's life:

  • 1983 - Sold his first video game at the age of 12.
  • 1990 - Attends Queen's University.
  • 1995 - Moved to California to attend Stanford for his PhD, but quit to develop Zip2.
  • 1999 - Sells Zip2 to Compaq
  • 2002 - Invests in SpaceX
  • 2004 - Starts Tesla Motors and creates the "Roadster"
  • 2013 - Work on high speed travel technology begins
